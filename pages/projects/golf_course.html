Lake Mira Mar Golf Course

The Course

Hole 1

Start the course with an easy hole. This is our widest green and one of the shortest. It may be an easy shot but don't get distacted by the crowd waiting for some good play.

Hole 2

After an easy is one of our hardest green. This is the longest green with 575 for our longest tee. Aim straight and avoid the breeze if you want to make this one!

Hole 3

This green is played like a mini course. Create an angled shot and make sure to hit strong enough for the ball to pass the incline. Hit too soft and the ball might just come back to where it started.

Hole 4

Curve the shot! This green is very tricky. Angle your shot carefully or else you'll find yourself on a bunker!

Hole 5

This is our second longest tee. All you have to do is aim straight but be very careful with the wind. You are up on a hit with this shot and anything can happen.

Hole 6

A simple tee but watch out for that curve.

Hole 7

This is our shortest tee. Aim straight and hit lightly and your on your way to the hole!

Hole 8

Very similar to our fourth tee but much more narrow. Becareful you dont hit people that are jogging.

Hole 9

Finish string with our easiest tee! Short and sweet hit should take the ball home.
